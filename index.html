Later, This Data can be sent to the database (Firebase) and then to the App using Flutter language for Remote monitoring purposes.

Air Quality Monitoring and Alert System

Our smart air quality monitoring system uses the ESP32 microcontroller and MQ2 gas sensor to detect harmful gases like smoke, LPG, and methane in real-time. The system sends immediate alerts when unsafe levels are detected, ensuring timely action. Data is transmitted to Firebase, where it's processed and stored securely. Users can access live air quality data, detailed analytics, and historical trends through a custom-built Flutter mobile app.

Technologies: ESP32, MQ2 Sensor, Firebase, Flutter

Year: 2025
